The Contest

Teams of two participated in this Creative Jam to complete a Graphic Design challenge using Adobe Creative Cloud tools. The theme of the challenge was "Vote", and teams were given 3 hours to create any medium using at least one of the following Adobe programs: Capture, Libraries, Dimension, Stock, Typekit, and/or Spark.
